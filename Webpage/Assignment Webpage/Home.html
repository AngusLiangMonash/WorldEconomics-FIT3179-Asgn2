For the year 2021, this visualisation will explore and compare the Macroeconomics that exists from country to country. This report will show and compare countries' Cost of Living, Annual Income, Corruption Index, GDP per Capita and Unemployment rate. The dataset, consisting attributes as mentioned, are provided in the Public database, and can be accessed from Kaggle. Annual Income is the amount of income a citizen earn in one fiscal year. The Corruption Index is an index which ranks countries by their perceived levels of public sector corruption. Cost Index is referred to the cost of living. Purchasing Power Index is the value of a currency expressed in terms of the number of goods or services that one unit of money can buy. GPD Per Capita is the sum of gross value added by all resident producers in the economy plus any product taxes (less subsidies) not included in the valuation of output, divided by mid-year population. Unemployment Rate is the percentage of people in the labour force who are unemployed  

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/madhurpant/world-economic-data

This report will not account for years prior of 2021; this is simply an annual report regarding the Macroeconomics statistics from a sample of countries.
